Balatro

Let me tell you about a little game called Balatro. Balatro is a simple playing card based computer game made by ONE hobbiest game-dev in his free time, where you create poker hands to score BIG points. For example, when you start a new run, the first blind requires you to get 300 points. 8 cards are then drawn from the deck, and in four playable hands, as well as the option to discard and re-draw up to five of your cards, you try to make the best poker hands possible to meet that score (for example, if you play a Straight, you'll score around 280 points, then you could finish it off with a simple pair or three of a kind, which would give you like 40-100 points). The fun part comes in where you then earn MONEY from beating that first requirement, which you can then use to buy JOKERS (you can have up to 5 by default) which have unique abilities that let you either increase your score, earn you extra money, make difficult-to-get hands easier to draw, and a LOT more. As a little nod to how unique some of these joker abilities, and the synergies between them get, there's this joker called Baron, which MULTIPLIES the score of your played hand by 1.5 for every King held in your hand while the hand is played. That, combined with the Mime joker, which retriggers every "held in hand" ability, will let you get CRAZY high scores. So if you played a Three of a Kind, and it were to score let's say 150 points, and you have five kings left in your hand with those two jokers equipped, each one of those kings would multiply your score by 1.5 TWICE, which would total up to a monumental 8,650 points. That's a little simplified explanation, but that's just a little glimpse into how well-thought-out every element of the game was implemented. Over the past two years of playing, I've accumulated over 600 hours in the game (my 3rd most played game ever), and have beaten the highest difficulty with all 150 jokers. I love this game very much, and I'd reccomend it to just about anyone.
