HTML tags I've learned

"p"
The paragraph tag. Sets up for a paragraph.
"abbr"
The abbreviation tag. Makes it so that when you hover over a specific part of the (abbreviated) text with your mouse, a small box showing the full name will appear (or whatever other text you wanna hide there)
"h1"
Creates a heading. This heading will have the highest priority when the search engine searches for keywords.
"em"
Emphasizes the selected piece of text.
"ul"
Creates a section for an unordered list.
